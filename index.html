Description

EVE - Isk per Hour is a Windows program that allows players of EVE Online to determine ways to maximize their Isk per Hour through manufacturing, mining, invention, and reverse engineering.

Please use this forum thread or email eveiskperhour@gmail.com to ask questions, report bugs, or provide feedback and feature requests. Thanks!
